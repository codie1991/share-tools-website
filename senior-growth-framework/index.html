Growth Reflection Exercise

Senior Engineer Growth Reflection

Complete all four parts below. Your scores update the radar automatically. When you're done, generate a markdown summary to bring to your manager 1:1.

Part 1 Your Growth Radar Baseline Rate yourself on all 8 dimensions

Use the 1–5 scale. Expand each dimension, pick your score — it updates the radar — then answer the two reflection prompts. Concrete examples beat long paragraphs.

Part 2 Archetype Exploration Identify the shape that interests you most

These archetypes aren't job titles or paths you must choose between — they're prompts to help you think about the kind of growth that interests you.

Select the archetype that resonates most
Part 3 Growth Focus Areas Pick 2–3 dimensions to focus on over the next 6–12 months

These don't have to be your lowest scores. Choose areas that are most energising, most aligned to where you want to go, or most relevant to the work ahead.

Part 4 OKRs Turn your focus areas into measurable objectives

One or two well-crafted OKRs are more useful than three vague ones. Key Results should describe outcomes, not tasks.

Ready to discuss? Generate a markdown summary to share with your manager or save alongside your notes.
